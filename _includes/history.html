History

Here is a little bit of history on the house.

  • 1944

    The House was Built

    All the houses in this area were built around that time in response to WWII and the G.I. Bill. Because of this, the same basic footprint is seen throughout the neighborhood, although you wouldn't see that at first glance as they have all been updated in one way or another over the past 75 years.

  • Pre 2004

    The house saw many different families before we entered the picture. Some loved the house, others not so much. It had been a rental for a number of years, everything was white and the finishes were cheap. You wouldn't recognize it from those pictures today.

  • July 1st, 2004

    Jacob Buys the House

    Under continuous improvement since, every room has been painted, updated, and improved. Many different housemates lived here over the years and they all would tell you this was one of the best homes they have ever lived in.

  • 2006-2009

    One Big Happy Family

    Jacob's Sister Kara's family moved in with 3 young children aged 3-8. This is when the name The Purple came to be.

  • 2014

    Katie!!!

    Katie moved in in 2014 and in 2015 we got married. Since the addition of Katie many wonderful projects have been undertaken including the basement apartment, the back patio, the main bathroom, and more.
